Read my plan about a card game:
We distribute a set of 5 cards to both the player 1 same visible card to both the player. First player has to do 2 things which is to take a single card either the visible one or from the shuffled deck (random card) and drop multiple card or a single card. Players can drop 2 cards simultaneously only if he has 2 same number card (king, king or 7, 7) or 3 cards together if he has a sequence (circular, Queen -> King -> Ace or  Ace -> 2 -> 3) or 4 cards together if he has same number or face card or 5 cards together if its a sequence or if they all belong to the same group. And the visible card gets replaced by all the card discarded by the player who played his turn. The previously exposed cards is kept aside till the hidden shuffled deck has no cards left. After that all the previously exposed cards is shuffled and kept as hidden card to pick from. By playing turn by turn, they've to minimize the sum of cards and when a player feels that he has the lowest, he can declare. If he was had the lowest, then the opponent gets the difference of their sum as score and the declarer gets 0. But if he declared and he didn't had the lowest score, he gets 20 penalty and difference of their score. Whichever player makes the other player score to 100 first, he wins. Each player gets a time of 60 seconds. If he doesn't play, the other player gets the turn.
